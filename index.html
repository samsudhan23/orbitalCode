FAQs

Have Questions?

We’re committed to making your experience as smooth and straightforward as possible.

What services does Orbital Code provide?

Orbital Code offers website and mobile app development for businesses of all sizes. We create custom websites, eCommerce platforms, and interactive mobile apps for iOS and Android. Our UI/UX design ensures visually appealing and user-friendly experiences. We also provide custom software solutions to optimize business operations. Our goal is to deliver scalable and high-performance digital products.

What technologies do you use for development?

We use Angular, React, Vue.js, and Node.js for web development. For mobile apps, we specialize in React Native, Flutter, Swift (iOS), and Kotlin (Android). Our backend services utilize MongoDB, MySQL, Firebase, and AWS for secure data handling. We focus on scalability, performance, and security in every project. Our tech stack ensures long-term reliability and efficiency.

How long does it take to develop a website or app?

Development time depends on features and complexity. A basic website takes 2 to 4 weeks, while an eCommerce site may take 6 to 12 weeks. Mobile apps typically take 8 to 16 weeks, depending on the platform and features. We follow an agile process to ensure timely delivery. Our focus is on creating high-quality and efficient solutions.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! We offer ongoing support and maintenance to keep your website or app running smoothly. Our services include bug fixes, security updates, and performance enhancements. We ensure your digital product stays updated and competitive. Our team is available for technical support and feature improvements. Your success is our priority!

How can I get started with Orbital Code?

Simply contact us with your project idea, and we’ll schedule a free consultation. We’ll discuss your requirements, budget, and timeline and create a development plan. Once approved, we start building your website or app with agile methodologies. After launch, we provide ongoing support and updates. Let’s build something amazing together!
